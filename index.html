About the Foundation

The Rajasthan Sports Foundation Trust is a dedicated sports and social welfare organization committed to nurturing young talent, promoting fitness, and creating positive social impact.

Founded by Chirag Choudhary, the foundation aims to provide equal opportunities for aspiring athletes by encouraging discipline, teamwork, leadership, and healthy lifestyles among youth from all sections of society.

"Our vision is empowering individuals through sports education, training programs, and social development initiatives that inspire confidence and unity within the community."

Our Services

We offer a range of programs designed to bridge the gap between passion and performance, ensuring sports becomes a medium for national building.

Elite Training

Dedicated sports education and high-intensity training programs for aspiring local athletes across multiple disciplines.

Awareness Campaigns

Regular workshops focused on health awareness, drug prevention, and the importance of active living for Jaipur youth.

Social Welfare

Active support for charitable activities, education support, and community welfare initiatives for marginalized sections.

Tournaments

Organizing local and state-level competitions to provide a platform for talent recognition and competitive exposure.

Leadership Camps

Short-term intensives focused on developing teamwork, discipline, and grit among the next generation of Rajasthan's leaders.

Health Support

Coordinating nutritional guidance and physical therapy support for athletes in our training programs.
